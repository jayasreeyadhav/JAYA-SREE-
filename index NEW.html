🔧 Mechanical Design · TIG Welding

TIG Filler Rod Holder with Kinematic Spring-Feed Mechanism

This project aims to enhance TIG welding efficiency by developing an innovative filler rod holder with an integrated kinematic spring-feed mechanism to automate manual feeding. Traditional techniques often cause operator fatigue and weld inconsistencies, which this ergonomic solution addresses by providing smoother, more controlled delivery of filler material.
